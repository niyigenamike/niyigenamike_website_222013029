Welcome to Green Academy Secondary School, where learning meets excellence. About Us

At Green Academy, we are committed to nurturing the intellectual, emotional, and social growth of our students. Our dedicated faculty members strive to create a supportive and inclusive learning environment where every student can reach their full potential. Through innovative teaching methods and personalized attention, we aim to cultivate lifelong learners and responsible citizens.

Our Courses

Our curriculum at Green Academy is designed to foster intellectual curiosity and academic excellence. We offer a diverse range of courses spanning various subjects, including mathematics, sciences, humanities, and the arts. Our experienced educators provide engaging and interactive lessons that challenge students to think critically and creatively.

Our Facilities

Green Academy is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities to support the educational and extracurricular needs of our students. From well-equipped classrooms and laboratories to expansive sports fields and recreational areas, we provide a conducive environment for learning, exploration, and personal growth. Our commitment to excellence extends to every aspect of our school community.
